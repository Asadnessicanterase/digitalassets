Stablecoins & Payments

Stablecoins are tokens designed to maintain a stable value relative to a reference asset such as the U.S. dollar【1060†L9-L10】. Policymakers view dollar‑backed stablecoins as the next wave of innovation in payments【880†L10-L13】.

Definition: A payment stablecoin is a digital asset designed to be used as a means of payment and to maintain a stable value relative to a fixed monetary amount【2370†L1-L6】.
